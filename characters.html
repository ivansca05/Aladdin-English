Characters

Aladdin:

Physical characteristics: Aladdin is a young, good-looking guy with a slim body, dark hair, and brown eyes. He usually wears simple clothes that a street kid would wear.

Emotional characteristics: Aladdin is clever, brave, and kind. Even though he starts out poor, he stays positive and always tries to do the right thing, especially when it comes to protecting the people he cares about.

Princess Jasmine:

Physical characteristics: Jasmine is a beautiful young woman with long black hair, big brown eyes, and a graceful body. She often wears elegant, royal clothes that show she’s a princess.

Emotional characteristics: Jasmine is independent, strong-minded, and smart. She isn’t afraid to say what she thinks and doesn’t want to just follow the rules that others try to make her follow. She values freedom and wants to make her own choices.

Genie:

Physical characteristics: The Genie is a big, blue magical being with a strong upper body and a lower half that looks like a tail coming out of his lamp. He has very expressive facial features and usually has a playful smile.

Emotional characteristics: The Genie is funny, clever, and loyal. He has a lot of power but is bound by the rules of the lamp. Despite these limits, he becomes good friends with Aladdin and uses his powers to help him in creative and fun ways.

Jafar:

Physical characteristics: Jafar is tall and thin with a scary look. He has a sharp face, a thin beard, and dark, intense eyes. He usually wears dark robes and carries a staff shaped like a snake.

Emotional characteristics: Jafar is sneaky, ambitious, and ruthless. He wants power and will do anything to get it, including lying and manipulating others. His cold and calculating nature makes him a strong villain. .
